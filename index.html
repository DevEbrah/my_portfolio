About Me

I'm Hibrahim Onyango — known online as Dev Ebrah or Jalmariek. I’m a Full-Stack Developer, Network Administrator & Systems Administrator with a background that extends beyond code into the infrastructure that powers it.

I understand what sits underneath applications — servers, networks, and the systems that keep everything running. That foundation shapes how I build software: not just for functionality in a local environment, but for reliability in real-world production systems.

I began in IT infrastructure and systems administration, learning how digital environments are designed, connected, and maintained from the ground up. Over time, I transitioned into software development, where I found my strength at the intersection of both worlds — building applications that are both functional and operationally sound.

⚙️ I think in systems — everything is a process waiting to be optimized
